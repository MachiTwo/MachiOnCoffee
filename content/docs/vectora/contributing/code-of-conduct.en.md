Vectora follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1, a code of conduct for inclusive and respectful open source communities.

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and leaders, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Show empathy towards other community members.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior can be reported to the project leaders responsible for enforcement at: **<conduct@vectora.app>**

All complainants will receive a prompt and fair response.
