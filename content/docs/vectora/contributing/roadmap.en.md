## Status Legend

- **Shipped**: Feature available in production.
- **In Progress / Beta**: Active development or limited beta testing.
- **Planned**: Approved and scheduled for development.
- **Under Discussion**: Open for community feedback and refinement.
- **On Hold**: Development temporarily paused.

## How to Vote on Features

1. Open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/Kaffyn/Vectora/discussions).
2. Use the `#feature-request` tag.
3. React with emojis on existing features.
4. Comment with your specific use cases.
